Introduction



Behold, Erlava! A mighty language forged in the fires of Java, yet imbued with the spirit of Erlang's concurrency. Erlava, a general-purpose language, rises from the ashes to conquer the realm of distributed applications. Built upon the bedrock of Java, Erlava leverages its robust libraries and tooling. But fear not, for Erlava speaks a different tongue, one inspired by Erlang's elegance for handling concurrency.

Interpreted by a cunning runtime, Erlava's code flows freely, unburdened by the shackles of compilation. An extensive standard library, a cornucopia of functionality, awaits your command. Embrace Erlava, and build applications that scale to the heavens, applications that dance across a multitude of machines, applications that are truly concurrent!
